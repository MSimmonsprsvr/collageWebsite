Top Courses

  • Wisdom 101The Truth of Yahweh's Light Explore the theological foundations of Yahweh's light, its significance in the Bible, and its role in dispelling darkness.
  • Wisdom 2Exegesis of Messianic Prophecies Analyze Old Testament prophecies related to the Messiah, exploring their fulfillment in the New Testament.
  • The 3rd WisdomThe Life and Teachings of Yahweh's Son Study the life, teachings, and ministry of Jesus Christ, focusing on his role as the Light of the world.
  • The 4th WisdomSpiritual Transformation and Renewal Examine the process of spiritual transformation and how faith in Yahweh's Son can lead to inner renewal and enlightenment.
  • The 5th WisdomBiblical Wisdom Literature Dive into the wisdom literature of the Bible, including Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and the Song of Solomon, and extract timeless wisdom for modern living.
  • The 6th WisdomThe Battle of Light and Darkness Explore the biblical narrative of the battle between light and darkness, examining how believers can use the light to combat evil forces.
  • The 7th WisdomTechinical in depth Training on Engaging with Intellectual Skepticism. Equip scholars with the tools to engage with intellectual skepticism and effectively defend their faith through rational discourse.
  • The 8th WisdomChristian Ethics and Moral Decision-Making Delve into the ethical teachings of Christianity and explore how they guide moral decision-making in contemporary society.
  • The 9th WisdomInterfaith Dialogue and Comparative Religion --HERE YOU WILL-- Foster understanding to respectfully dialogue between different faiths while deepening the appreciation of one's own beliefs as you create opportunities to convert and illuminate spirit, soul, and body.
  • The 10th WisdomThe Art of Evangelism and Transformation Explore strategies for sharing the message of Yahweh's Son and the transformative power of faith with intellectual skeptics and seekers.

    • These courses offer a comprehensive exploration of biblical wisdom, faith, and spirituality, appealing to seasoned scholars and seekers alike. The curriculum combines rigorous theological study with practical applications, equipping students with the knowledge and tools to engage in meaningful spiritual growth and outreach.
